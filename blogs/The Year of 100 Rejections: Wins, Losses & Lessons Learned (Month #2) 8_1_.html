The Year of 100 Rejections: Wins, Losses & Lessons Learned (Month #2)

I just completed my second month of the The Year of 100 Rejections!

If you’re new, this is the project where I’m trying to learn how to write fiction by collecting 100 short story rejections.

First, some data for this month:

  • This month, I wrote 14,760 words, or 59 pages of fiction.
  • On average, that’s 547 words—or 2.2 pages—per day.
  • I started 5 stories this month and finished 2 of them.
  • I submitted the 2 finished stories a total of 2 times.
  • From submitted stories, I received 3 rejections this month.
Goal progress: 6/100 rejections.
